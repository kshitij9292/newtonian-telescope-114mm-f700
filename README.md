🛠️ Bill of Materials (BOM)
1. Optics

    Primary Mirror: 114mm diameter, 700mm focal length.

    Secondary Mirror: ~25mm - 28mm minor axis elliptical mirror.

2. Structural Hardware

    Truss Tubes: Pultruded Square Carbon Fiber Tube (Hollow)

        Dimensions: 10mm (OD) × 8mm (ID) × 1000mm (L)

        Quantity: 3

    Linear Rails: 8mm Smooth Rod

        Dimensions: 8mm diameter × 500mm length

    Rail Support: SHF8 8mm Linear Guide Rod Support (Horizontal Bracket)

    Fasteners:

        M4 x 20mm Hex bolts

        M8 x 30mm Hex bolts

        M5 x 40mm Hex bolts

    Threaded Inserts: M4 and M8 brass inserts (Heat-set).

🖨️ Printing Guidelines

    Material: PETG, ASA, or ABS.

       CRITICAL: Avoid PLA. It will warp under direct sunlight or in a warm vehicle, which permanently ruins optical alignment (collimation).

    Infill: 40% (Gyroid or Cubic recommended for maximum rigidity).

    Wall Loops: 4 minimum (Ensures strength for the heat-set inserts and SHF8 mounting points).

🔧 Assembly & Collimation

    Insert Hardware: Heat-set the brass inserts into the printed parts. Ensure the SHF8 brackets are mounted to a perfectly flat surface 
    Primary Cell: Mount the 114mm mirror into the cell using the printed clips.

    Do not overtighten the clips; "pinching" the mirror causes astigmatism (distorted star shapes).

    Collimation: * Adjust the three M8 screws on the primary cell to center the reflection.
